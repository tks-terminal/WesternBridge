flow of the game kinda
start travel > action for the day
actions include

1.Travel
 -move closer to destination
 -chance of random event
 -uses food 2

2.Rest
 -recovers health
 -uses food 1
 -no distance progress
 -repairs wagon

3.Hunt
 -uses food 1
 -high chance to gain food
 -low chance to lose health

4.Scavenge
 -uses food 1
 -chance to find medicine and small ammounts of food
 -smaller reward than hunting but no chance of getting hurt
 -chance to find money

Variables include
  1.DistanceAway
  2.Health
  3.wagon_health
  4.FoodAmount
  5.MedicineAmount
  6.GoldAmount

win by completing the distance
lose by running out of wagon health or player health



Classes:

1. Character class
   Properties:
   - Name
   - Health
   - IsAlive

   Methods:
   - TakeDamage(amount)
   - Heal(amount)
   - DisplayStats()

2. Player class : Character
   Properties:
   - DistanceAway
   - FoodAmount
   - MedicineAmount
   - WagonHealth
   - GoldAmount

   Methods:
   - Travel()
   - Rest()
   - Hunt()
   - Scavenge()
   - UseMedicine()

3. Scenario class
   Purpose:
   - Stores a random event and applies its effects

   Properties:
   - Description
   - IsGood
   - EffectType
   - EffectAmount

   Methods:
   - ApplyToPlayer()
   - DisplayScenario()

4. Game class
   Properties:
   - Player CurrentPlayer
   - List Scenarios
   - bool IsRunning

   Methods:
   - LoadScenariosFromFile()
   - StartGame()
   - ShowMenu()
   - TriggerRandomScenario()
   - CheckGameOver()
