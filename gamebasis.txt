flow of the game kinda
start travel > action for the day
actions include

1.Travel
 -move closer to destination
 -chance of random event
 -uses food 2

2.Rest
 -recovers health
 -uses food 1
 -no distance progress
 -repairs wagon

3.Hunt
 -uses food 1
 -high chance to gain food
 -low chance to lose health

4.Scavenge
 -uses food 1
 -chance to find medicine and small ammounts of food
 -smaller reward than hunting but no chance of getting hurt
 -chance to find money

Variables include
  1.DistanceAway
  2.Health
  3.wagon_health
  4.FoodAmount
  5.MedicineAmount
  6.GoldAmount

win by completing the distance
lose by running out of wagon health or player health
