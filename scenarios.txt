# GOOD SCENARIOS

1. You find extra food in an abandoned wagon. +3 FoodAmount
2. Your group discovers a shortcut through the hills. -2 DistanceAway
3. A trader gives you a fair deal on supplies. +2 FoodAmount, +2 MedicineAmount
4. You find fresh water near camp. +2 Health
5. A kind stranger helps repair your wagon. +3 WagonHealth
6. You hunt successfully and gain food. +4 FoodAmount
7. Your party finds a safe place to rest. +2 Health, +1 WagonHealth
8. You discover a hidden trail. -2 DistanceAway
9. You meet settlers who share supplies. +2 FoodAmount, +1 MedicineAmount
10. You safely cross a dangerous pass. +2 Health, -1 DistanceAway


# BAD SCENARIOS

11. A river crossing damages your supplies. -2 FoodAmount, -2 WagonHealth
12. A storm slows your travel for two days. +2 DistanceAway
13. Bandits steal some of your food at night. -3 FoodAmount, -2 GoldAmount
14. One of your oxen gets injured. -2 WagonHealth
15. You get lost and waste valuable time. +3 DistanceAway
16. A broken bridge forces a detour. +2 DistanceAway, -2 WagonHealth
17. Heavy rain ruins some of your supplies. -2 FoodAmount, -1 MedicineAmount
18. A snake bites one of your travelers. -3 Health, -1 MedicineAmount
19. A wheel breaks on rough terrain. -3 WagonHealth
20. Cold weather weakens the group. -2 Health, -1 FoodAmount
