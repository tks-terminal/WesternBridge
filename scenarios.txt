# GOOD SCENARIOS

1. You find extra food in an abandoned wagon.
2. Your group discovers a shortcut through the hills.
3. A trader gives you a fair deal on supplies.
4. You find fresh water near camp.
5. A kind stranger helps repair your wagon.
6. You hunt successfully and gain food.
7. Your party finds a safe place to rest.
8. You discover a hidden trail.
9. You meet settlers who share supplies.
10. You safely cross a dangerous pass.


# BAD SCENARIOS

11. A river crossing damages your supplies.
12. A storm slows your travel for two days.
13. Bandits steal some of your food at night.
14. One of your oxen gets injured.
15. You get lost and waste valuable time.
16. A broken bridge forces a detour.
17. Heavy rain ruins some of your supplies.
18. A snake bites one of your travelers.
19. A wheel breaks on rough terrain.
20. Cold weather weakens the group.
